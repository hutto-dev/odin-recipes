Macaroni & Cheese

Description

This delicious recipe of homemade macaroni and cheese is exactly what you've been looking for. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 pot
  • 3 cups of water
  • Macaroni
  • Sharp Cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Bring your water to a boil.
  2. Add the macaroni
  3. Let the macaroni cook until soft
  4. Drain the water
  5. Add in the sharp cheddar cheese, the butter, and the milk
  6. Enjoy the best macaroni and cheese you've ever had.
