Root Beer Float

Description

One of the most delicious desserts you'll ever have. Hungry? No worries! Thirsty? No worries! Two for one!

Ingredients

  • Cup
  • Root beer
  • Vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Put 2-3 scoops of vanilla ice cream in your cup
  2. Pour the root beer over the ice cream
  3. Enjoy with a straw and/or spoon
